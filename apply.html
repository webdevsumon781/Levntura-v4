UIUX
DESIGNER

Computer science
22 Jan,2024 - 01 Jan,2025

Lithe Transformation is working with an international design consultancy on a project in Saudi Arabia. We are looking for UX/UI Designers (Luma qualification an advantage) to work with us. This is not a Front-End designer role. We are looking for people to implement service design from a higher-level product angle.


You can be based in the UK/Europe, but will need to be able to go to Saudi Arabia for short periods (weeks rather than days) to complete packets of work. Preference will also be given to candidates who can speak and write Arabic.


This project is only at proposal stage at the moment, but we need to have candidates at our disposal for when this goes live, which could be anytime in April or May. At this stage contract length is uncertain but we estimate 3-6 months with possible extension.


Although we like to publish rates on our job specs, this is a new skillset for us, so we appreciate that we will have to be driven by the market.

6595$/m

Requirements

  • 2-5 years of experience in UI/UX design, with a specific focus on DTC brands in the health and wellness sector.
  • Demonstrated experience with no-code website builders and a strong understanding of the principles of design and user experience.
  • Proven track record of creating visually appealing designs that align with brand guidelines and marketing objectives.
  • Experience in conducting A/B testing and using data analytics to inform design decisions.
  • Strong communication skills, with the ability to articulate design concepts and decisions clearly.
  • Independent, proactive, and adaptable, with a startup mentality and the ability to thrive in a dynamic environment.

Benifits

Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)

Life insurance

Disability insurance

Paid time off (vacation, sick leave, holidays) 401(k)
retirement plan with employer matching
